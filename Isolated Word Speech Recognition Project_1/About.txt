About this project:

This project is about Isolated Word Speech Recognition. 
It means the trained model can identify our speech or voice and recognize it. Yeah it is awesome.

Prerequired:

    Dataset used in this project: http://download.tensorflow.org/data/speech_commands_v0.02.tar.gz

Modules used:

    tensorflow	2.6.0
    numpy	1.19.5
    librosa	0.8.1
    matplotlib	3.4.3
    ipython	7.26.0
